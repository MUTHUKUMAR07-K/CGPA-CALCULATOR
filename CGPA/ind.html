NEC CGPA CALCULATOR

Steps To Calculate CGPA

STEP 1: Enter the Number of Semesters correctly based on this calculation will done.

STEP 2: For Each Semester, Enter the Numbers of Subjects.

STEP 3: Enter the Relevant Semester Subjects Course code(Optional),GRADE and Credit from the List.

STEP 4: After entering all subjects information, Press Calculate to Know the CGPA

STEP 5: The Result will contains
  • GPA for Each Semester in Percentage(%) out of 10
  • CGPA for Overall Semester in Percentage(%) out of 10
  • Total Credits Earned for all the Semester

NOTE if you have an Arrear, Your not Elgible to Calculate CGPA
